Eddie Morrison is an Australia-based actor, writer, and voiceover artist.

He has worked extensively on stage, most notably for State Theatre Company SA, Patch Theatre Company, and State Opera SA. He is also a founding member of Zero Theatre Collective, and a leading exponent of 'Jokerfied Insane Clown Theatre'.

His film work includes numerous short and full-length films, most recently A Day For Losers for JG Clarke Productions.

Eddie is also an emerging playwright; his first full-length play, Paladin, is due for production in 2026.
